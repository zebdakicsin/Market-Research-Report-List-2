<p><p>A wireless charging lamp is a type of lighting fixture that integrates wireless charging technology, allowing users to charge their smartphones, smartwatches, and other compatible devices by simply placing them on the lamp base or surface. These lamps provide a clutter-free charging experience, eliminating the need for cords or cables.
