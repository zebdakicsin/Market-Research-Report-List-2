<p><ul><li>Whey Protein Concentrate</li><li>Whey Protein Isolate</li><li>Whey Protein Hydrostate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pure whey protein market can be categorized into three main types: whey protein concentrate, whey protein isolate, and whey protein hydrostate. Whey protein concentrate is the most common form, containing around 70-80% protein with small amounts of lactose and fat. Whey protein isolate undergoes additional processing, yielding a higher protein content of around 90-95% with minimal lactose and fat. Finally, whey protein hydrostate is enzymatically hydrolyzed for easier digestion and faster absorption by the body. These different types cater to different needs and preferences of consumers seeking protein supplementation.</p></p>
