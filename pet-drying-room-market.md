<p><p>The pet drying room market offers various types of equipment to efficiently dry pets after bathing. The plain fan type employs a simple fan system to circulate air and dry the pets. Axial type uses axial fans to move the air in a linear path. Turbine type comprises turbines that generate high pressure to dry animals quickly. Direct exhaust type has an exhaust system to remove moist air from the room. Hybrid type combines different technologies, such as fans and air filters, to provide a versatile drying solution. These types cater to different customer needs and preferences.</p></p>
