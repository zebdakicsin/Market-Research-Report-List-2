<p><p>The Composite Solar Backsheet market includes three main types of back sheets: Double-Sided Fluorine Film Backplane, Single-Sided Fluorine Film Backplane, and Fluorine-Free Backplane. Double-Sided Fluorine Film Backplane refers to back sheets with fluorine film on both sides, offering excellent weather resistance and durability. Single-Sided Fluorine Film Backplane has fluorine film on one side, which provides good barrier properties but at a lower cost. Fluorine-Free Backplane is an environmentally friendly alternative without the use of fluorine, ensuring sustainability without compromising on performance.</p></p>
