<p><p>Aloe extract is commonly divided into two types: Aloe gel extracts and Aloe whole leaf extracts. Aloe gel extracts are obtained by processing the inner gel of the Aloe vera plant. These extracts are known for their moisturizing and healing properties and are widely used in skincare products and cosmetics. On the other hand, Aloe whole leaf extracts are derived from the entire leaf of the Aloe vera plant and contain a higher concentration of nutrients and bioactive compounds. These extracts are commonly used in dietary supplements and health products. Both types of Aloe extracts have their unique benefits and applications in different industries.</p></p>
