<p><p>The Electric Griddle market offers two types of controls: Manual Control and Thermostatic Control. In Manual Control griddles, the operator adjusts the temperature manually by turning a knob or dial. These griddles are simple to use but require constant monitoring to maintain the desired temperature. On the other hand, Thermostatic Control griddles have built-in thermostats that automatically regulate the temperature. This type offers more precise temperature control and is preferred in commercial settings where consistency is crucial. Both types cater to different preferences and requirements of users within the Electric Griddle market.</p></p>
