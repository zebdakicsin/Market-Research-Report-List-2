<p><strong>The Aerospace Grade Acrylic Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Acrylic Cast Plate</li><li>Acrylic Extrusion Sheet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aerospace grade acrylic market includes two major types: acrylic cast plate and acrylic extrusion sheet. Acrylic cast plate refers to a type of aerospace grade acrylic formed by pouring liquid acrylic resin into a mold and allowing it to harden. It is known for its superior strength, impact resistance, and clarity. On the other hand, acrylic extrusion sheet is manufactured by passing heated acrylic resin through a die, resulting in a solid sheet with uniform thickness. It is valued for its excellent transparency, UV resistance, and versatility in aerospace applications.</p></p>
