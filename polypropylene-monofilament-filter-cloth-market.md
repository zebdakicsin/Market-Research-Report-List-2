Double weave utilizes two layers of yarns, providing enhanced filtration efficiency. Three-layer weave involves three layers of monofilaments woven together to offer improved durability and filtration performance. Other fabric types encompass unique weaves or combinations that cater to specific filtration needs. These different types accommodate diverse filtration demands in industries such as pharmaceuticals, food and beverages, automotive, and wastewater treatment.</p></p>
