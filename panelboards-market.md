<p><p>Panelboards are electrical distribution boards used to control and distribute power feed to various electrical loads. The market is divided into two main types: distribution and lighting panelboards, and appliance panelboards.</p><p>Distribution or lighting panelboards are primarily used to control and distribute power for general lighting and power circuits in commercial and residential buildings. They feature circuit breakers or fuses to protect against overload and short circuits.</p><p>Appliance panelboards, on the other hand, are designed specifically to supply power to appliances and equipment in industrial and commercial settings. They often include motor starters and specialized controls to handle the unique power requirements of appliances and machinery.</p><p>Both types of panelboards play a crucial role in ensuring efficient and safe distribution of electrical power in different applications.</p></p>
