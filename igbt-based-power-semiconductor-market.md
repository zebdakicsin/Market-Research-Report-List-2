<p><p>The IGBT-based power semiconductor market can be divided into two main types: discrete and module. </p><p>Discrete IGBTs are individual components that can perform power switching functions. They are commonly used in applications that require lower power levels and can be easily replaced or repaired. </p><p>Module IGBTs, on the other hand, are integrated packages consisting of multiple discrete IGBTs along with other necessary components like diodes and gate drivers. These modules are generally used in applications that require high power levels and offer advantages such as compactness, higher efficiency, and improved thermal management.</p></p>
