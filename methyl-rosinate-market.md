<p><p>The Methyl Rosinate market is divided into two main types: Type I and Type II. Type I market refers to the category that includes pure or natural methyl rosinates derived from the gum rosin. It is commonly used in the manufacturing of adhesives, coatings, and printing inks. On the other hand, Type II market involves modified or semi-synthetic methyl rosinates that are obtained by treating the natural gum rosin with certain chemicals. This type is primarily utilized in the production of varnishes, sealants, and soldering fluxes.</p></p>
