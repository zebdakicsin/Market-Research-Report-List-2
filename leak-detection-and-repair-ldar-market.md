<p><p>Leak Detection and Repair (LDAR) market involves various types of technologies. Portable analyzers are handheld devices used to detect leaks by analyzing the composition of gases. Optical gas imaging employs infrared cameras to visualize gas leaks. Laser absorption spectroscopy measures gas concentrations by laser beam absorption. Ambient/mobile leak monitoring uses fixed or moving sensors to detect leaks continuously. Acoustic leak detectors identify leaks by analyzing sound frequency patterns. Lastly, there are other miscellaneous technologies used in LDAR, such as sniffer devices or gas chromatography. These technologies aid in detecting and repairing leaks in industries like oil and gas, chemical manufacturing, and others.</p></p>
