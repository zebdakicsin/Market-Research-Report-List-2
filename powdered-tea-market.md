<p><p>In the powdered tea market, there are two main types: drinking-use tea and additive-use tea. Drinking-use tea refers to powdered tea that is intended to be mixed with hot water or milk and consumed as a beverage. It includes popular varieties like matcha and green tea powder. Additive-use tea, on the other hand, is powdered tea that is used as an ingredient or additive in various food and beverage products. It is often used to enhance flavors or add health benefits to products like smoothies, desserts, or energy bars.</p></p>
