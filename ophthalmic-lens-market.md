<p><p>The ophthalmic lens market consists of several types, including ADC ophthalmic lens, PC ophthalmic lens, PU ophthalmic lens, acrylic ophthalmic lens, epoxy ophthalmic lens, and others. ADC lenses are made from allyl diglycol carbonate and offer excellent optical properties. PC lenses are made from polycarbonate and are known for their impact resistance. PU lenses are made from polyurethane and provide superior optical clarity. Acrylic lenses are made from polymethyl methacrylate and are lightweight and durable. Epoxy lenses are made from epoxy resin and are highly resistant to chemicals. The "others" category includes various lens materials such as glass, trivex, and high-index lenses.</p></p>
