Cantilever probes consist of spring-loaded wires that enable precise and repeatable probing. Vertical probes are mostly used for testing bare chips and possess high-density and high-speed capabilities. The "Others" category includes specialized probes such as Kelvin probes for measuring voltage and current simultaneously.
