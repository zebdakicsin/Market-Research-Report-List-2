<p><p>The thermal compound and grease market can be classified into three types: metal-based, ceramic-based, and carbon-based. Metal-based thermal compounds are made of small metal particles mixed with a carrier fluid. They provide excellent thermal conductivity and are commonly used in high-performance applications. Ceramic-based compounds are composed of ceramic particles suspended in a liquid or grease. They offer good electrical insulation and high-temperature resistance but have lower thermal conductivity. Carbon-based compounds contain carbon particles in a base material and are known for their non-conductive properties and easy application.</p></p>
