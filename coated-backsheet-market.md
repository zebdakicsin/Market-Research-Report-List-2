<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The coated backsheet market consists of two main types: fluoropolymer and non-fluoropolymer. Fluoropolymer backsheet is made from materials like polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE) and offers excellent resistance to heat, chemicals, and UV radiation. It is widely used in demanding applications and high-performance solar panels. On the other hand, non-fluoropolymer backsheet is made from materials like polyvinyl fluoride (PVF) or polyethylene terephthalate (PET). These backsheet types provide good electrical insulation and weather resistance but may not have the same level of durability as fluoropolymer options.</p></p>
