<p><p>Piezoelectric pumps are a type of pump that utilizes the piezoelectric effect to generate pressure and produce flow. These pumps rely on the electrical charge generated by certain materials, such as crystals or ceramics, when subjected to mechanical stress, to generate fluid propulsion. This technology has gained significant traction in various industries such as medical, automotive, aerospace, and consumer electronics due to its small size, precise control, and ability to operate under extreme conditions.</p><p>The piezoelectric pumps market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.5% during the forecast period. These pumps offer higher efficiency and precise fuel metering, resulting in improved vehicle performance and reduced emissions.</p><p>Another trend in the piezoelectric pumps market is the development of advanced materials and manufacturing processes.
