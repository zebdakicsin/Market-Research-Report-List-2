<p><strong>The General Purpose Resistors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SMD Type</li><li>Through Hole Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The general purpose resistors market is categorized into two types: SMD (Surface Mount Device) Type and Through Hole Type. SMD Type resistors are small in size and designed for surface mounting on printed circuit boards, allowing for efficient space utilization, high-density assembly, and automated manufacturing processes. On the other hand, Through Hole Type resistors are larger and have leads that are inserted into holes on the circuit board, offering robustness and ease of manual soldering. Both types serve the general purpose of providing resistance in electronic circuits but differ in their form factor and assembly methods.</p></p>
