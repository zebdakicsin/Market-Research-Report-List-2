<p><p>The whipped cream stabilizer market is classified into different types, including starch type, xanthan gum type, guar gum type, and others. The starch type stabilizers are derived from corn or potato starch and help enhance the texture and stability of whipped cream. Xanthan gum stabilizers are made from bacteria fermentation and provide thickening and stabilizing properties. Guar gum stabilizers, derived from guar beans, are known for their ability to improve the texture and viscosity of whipped cream. The "others" category includes stabilizers that are made from various ingredients, such as gelatin or carrageenan.</p></p>
