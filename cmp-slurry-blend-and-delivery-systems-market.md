<p><ul><li>Vessel Type (N2 Pressurization)</li><li>Tank Type (Pump Supply)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CMP (Chemical Mechanical Planarization) Slurry Blend and Delivery Systems market offers two main types: Vessel Type (N2 Pressurization) and Tank Type (Pump Supply). In the Vessel Type, slurry blend and delivery systems use pressurized N2 gas to move the slurry from the vessel to the polishing pad. On the other hand, in the Tank Type, a pump system is employed to supply the slurry from a tank to the polishing pad. These systems are designed to provide efficient and controlled delivery of slurry during CMP processes in the semiconductor and electronics industries.</p></p>
