<p><p>The advanced energy storage market encompasses various types of storage technologies for effectively managing energy supply and demand. Pumped hydro storage involves using excess energy to pump water uphill, which is then released to generate electricity when needed. Battery storage involves storing electricity in batteries for later use. Flywheel storage uses a spinning rotor to store kinetic energy, which can be converted back to electricity as required. Thermal storage involves storing and releasing heat energy. Other types include compressed air, hydrogen, and superconducting magnetic energy storage systems.</p></p>
