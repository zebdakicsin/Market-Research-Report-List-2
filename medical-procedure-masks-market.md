<p><p>The medical procedure masks market includes two main types: surgical masks and procedure masks. Surgical masks are designed to protect healthcare professionals and patients from exposure to infectious agents, and are typically fluid-resistant and provide a high filtration efficiency. Procedure masks, on the other hand, are mainly used by non-healthcare individuals to protect against respiratory droplets, dust, and other contaminants. While surgical masks are regulated by health authorities and have specific standards to meet, procedure masks are not subject to the same level of regulation.</p></p>
