<p><p>Sheathed cable refers to a type of electrical wiring that is encased in a protective covering. There are two main market types of sheathed cable: nonmetallic-sheathed and metallic-sheathed. Nonmetallic-sheathed cable is made of thermoplastic materials and is commonly used for residential wiring applications. It provides insulation and protection against moisture and is easier to install due to its flexibility. On the other hand, metallic-sheathed cable is constructed with a metal jacket that provides increased protection against physical damage and fire hazards. It is commonly used in commercial and industrial settings where durability is critical.</p></p>
