<p><p>The rubber hose market for the automotive industry consists of three main types: low pressure hose, high pressure hose, and oil-resistant hose. Low pressure hoses are primarily used in systems that operate at pressures below 100 psi, such as fuel and coolant systems. High pressure hoses, on the other hand, are designed to withstand pressures above 1,000 psi and are used in hydraulic systems. Oil-resistant hoses are specifically made to handle fluids containing oils and are commonly found in engine oil and transmission applications.</p></p>
