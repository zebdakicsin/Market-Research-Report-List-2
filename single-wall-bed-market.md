<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Single Wall Bed market encompasses three types: Straight Reverse, Incline, and Reverse. Straight Reverse wall beds are designed to be pulled down in a straight line and slide back up in the same manner. Incline wall beds, on the other hand, are installed at an angle and descend diagonally, providing a more inclined sleeping position. Lastly, Reverse wall beds are fixed at an angle and pull down in a straight line but rise up at an inclined angle. These variations cater to different customer preferences, providing options for various sleeping positions.</p></p>
