<p><strong>The Fiber Optic Sensing Solutions Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Distributed Sensing</li><li>Fiber Optic Point Sensing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fiber optic sensing solutions can be classified into two main types: distributed sensing and fiber optic point sensing. </p><p>Distributed sensing involves the continuous monitoring of physical parameters along the entire length of an optical fiber. This technology allows for real-time detection of changes in temperature, strain, pressure, or vibrations, enabling applications such as structural health monitoring and oil/gas pipeline monitoring.</p><p>On the other hand, fiber optic point sensing refers to detecting physical parameters at specific points along the fiber. This technology is often used in applications like perimeter security, oil well monitoring, and temperature sensing in power transformers.</p></p>
