The Classic Bluetooth Chip is designed for traditional Bluetooth audio streaming, offering higher data transfer rates and longer range connectivity. On the other hand, the BLE Chip is optimized for low-power devices, allowing longer battery life and enabling features like streaming music from smartphones with minimal impact on power consumption. These different types cater to different needs in the Bluetooth Speaker Chip market, providing options for both robust audio streaming and energy-efficient connectivity.</p></p>
