<p><strong>The 3D Sensing Array Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Structured Light</li><li>Time of Flight</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D sensing array market consists of two types: structured light and time of flight. Structured light technology projects a series of complex patterns onto an object to measure its depth and structure by analyzing deformations in the pattern. On the other hand, time of flight technology calculates the distance to an object by measuring the time it takes for a light signal to bounce back. Both technologies are widely used in various applications such as facial recognition, augmented reality, and autonomous vehicles.</p></p>
