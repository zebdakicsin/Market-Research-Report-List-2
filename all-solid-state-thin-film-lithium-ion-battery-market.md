<p><p>The all solid-state thin-film lithium-ion battery market includes two main types: polymer electrolyte thin film lithium battery and inorganic solid electrolyte thin film lithium battery. The polymer electrolyte thin film lithium battery utilizes a polymer-based electrolyte to facilitate the movement of lithium ions between the electrodes, while the inorganic solid electrolyte thin film lithium battery uses solid-state inorganic materials to achieve the same purpose. Both types offer advantages such as improved safety, longer cycle life, and higher energy density compared to traditional lithium-ion batteries.</p></p>
