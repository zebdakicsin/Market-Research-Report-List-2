<p><p>The market for oat flakes can be divided into three main types: instant oats, quick oats, and steel-cut oats. Instant oats are pre-cooked and then dried, making them convenient and quick to prepare. Quick oats are also partially cooked but retain a slightly chewy texture. Lastly, steel-cut oats are the least processed, with the whole grain cut into small pieces, resulting in a nuttier flavor and longer cooking time. These different types cater to various consumer preferences, offering a range of cooking times and textures to suit individual needs.</p></p>
