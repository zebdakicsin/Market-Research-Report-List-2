<p><p>Methyl Tert-Butyl Ether (MTBE) is a colorless, volatile liquid compound that is primarily used as a fuel additive in gasoline. The wide usage of MTBE in various applications, such as gasoline blending, solvents, and chemical intermediates, further drives its market growth. Dehydromethyl tert butyl ether is produced by removing water from MTBE, while Isomerized methyl tert butyl ether is created by isomerization.
