<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Dielectric paste is classified into two types: curing paste and sintered paste. Curing paste is a type of dielectric paste that is used in the fabrication of electronic components. It contains a polymer that can be cured under heat or UV light, forming a solid insulating layer. On the other hand, sintered paste is a dielectric paste that is used in the manufacturing of ceramic components. It consists of fine ceramic particles mixed with a binder, which is then sintered at high temperatures to create a dense and uniform insulating material.</p></p>
