<p><p>Organic Light Emitting Diode (OLED) Displays come in various types such as Active-Matrix OLED (AMOLED) Display and Passive Matrix OLED (PMOLED). AMOLED displays use a thin film transistor (TFT) to control each pixel individually, resulting in high resolution and better color accuracy. They are commonly used in smartphones and TVs. On the other hand, PMOLED displays use a simpler matrix of pixels, making them suitable for smaller devices like wearable gadgets. They offer lower resolution and limited color options but consume less power, making them ideal for simpler applications.</p></p>
