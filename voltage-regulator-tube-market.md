<p><p>The voltage-regulator tube market consists of two types: ferroresonant and tap switching. Ferroresonant voltage regulators use a magnetic resonance circuit to stabilize the voltage output. They are reliable and suitable for sensitive devices. On the other hand, tap switching voltage regulators operate by switching taps on the transformer winding to regulate the output voltage. They are cost-effective and commonly used in industrial applications. Both types play a vital role in maintaining a stable voltage supply in various electrical systems, ensuring optimal performance and protection for connected devices.</p></p>
