Two common types of hair brush straighteners are flat hair brush straighteners and rotating hair brush straighteners. Flat hair brush straighteners consist of a paddle-like brush with heated plates that glide through the hair, applying heat to straighten and smooth it. On the other hand, rotating hair brush straighteners have bristles that rotate, mimicking the action of a traditional hairbrush while straightening the hair. Both types offer efficient and effective straightening results.</p></p>
