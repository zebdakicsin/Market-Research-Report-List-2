<p><ul><li>Blanched Almond Powder</li><li>Natural Almond Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The almond powder market can be categorized into two types: blanched almond powder and natural almond powder. Blanched almond powder is made from almonds that have had their skins removed, resulting in a smooth and light-colored powder. It is commonly used in baking and cooking. Natural almond powder is made from whole almonds, including the skins, giving it a slightly darker color and a more intense flavor. This type of almond powder is often used in desserts and beverages. Both varieties are popular among consumers and offer different qualities for various culinary applications.</p></p>
