<p><p>Portable restrooms or mobile toilets are categorized into two main types: Lifting or Handling Moving Restrooms and Power or Trailer Mobile Toilets. Lifting or Handling Moving Restrooms refer to portable restrooms that are designed to be lifted or easily moved from one location to another, typically using specialized equipment. On the other hand, Power or Trailer Mobile Toilets are portable restrooms that are fully equipped with power sources, enabling them to operate independently. They are typically mounted on trailers and are suitable for events or construction sites where a continuous power supply is required.</p></p>
