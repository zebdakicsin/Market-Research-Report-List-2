<p><p>Lighting ballasts are devices used in fluorescent lamps to regulate the lamp's electrical current. The market types of lighting ballasts can be categorized into three: Preheat Operation Lamp Electrodes, Rapid Start Operation Lamp Electrodes, and Instant Start Operation Lamp Electrodes. Preheat operation lamp electrodes require a separate starter to ignite the lamp, while rapid start operation lamp electrodes have filament electrodes that are preheated, enabling them to start quickly. Instant start operation lamp electrodes have electrodes that are not preheated, resulting in the fastest starting time.</p></p>
