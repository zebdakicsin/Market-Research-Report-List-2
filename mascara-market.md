<p><p>The mascara market is divided into three main types: regular, waterproof, and water-resistant. Regular mascara is the most common type and is suitable for everyday use. Waterproof mascara is designed to withstand water, making it ideal for activities like swimming or crying. It forms a protective layer on the lashes that cannot be easily removed with water alone. Water-resistant mascara offers a compromise between regular and waterproof formulas. While it is not completely waterproof, it can withstand minimal exposure to water without smudging or running.</p></p>
