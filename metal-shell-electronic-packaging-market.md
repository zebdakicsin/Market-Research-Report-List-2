On the other hand, the Silicon Carbide Heat Sink market uses silicon carbide as the heat transfer material. It offers high thermal conductivity, enabling effective heat dissipation in electronic devices. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining optimal temperature levels to ensure smooth functioning and longevity of electronic components.</p></p>
