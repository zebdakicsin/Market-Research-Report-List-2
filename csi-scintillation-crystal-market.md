<p><p>CsI Scintillation Crystal Market Types refer to the different variations of crystals used in scintillation detectors. CsI(Tl) utilizes thallium as an activator, emitting blue light when exposed to radiation, making it suitable for gamma-ray and X-ray detection. CsI(Na), on the other hand, uses sodium as an activator, emitting yellow light. CsI(Tl) is preferred for higher energy range detection, while CsI(Na) is commonly used in low energy or high flux applications. Both crystals offer efficient and reliable detection capabilities in various scientific and industrial fields.</p></p>
